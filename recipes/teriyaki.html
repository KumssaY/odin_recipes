Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers

A tempting image displays skewers of Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple, with succulent shrimp coated in a glossy teriyaki glaze and golden pineapple chunks, creating a mouthwatering combination of flavors.

These savory-sweet shrimp skewers are easy to make and cook in just a few minutes. They are perfect for summer weeknights or weekend dinner parties. Prep the sauce ahead of time for an even faster dinner. I prefer to use jumbo shrimp (21 to 25 per pound), however use what you have on hand.

Directions

  1. Combine water, soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, garlic, and ginger in a saucepan.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  4. Preheat the outdoor grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  5. Thread shrimp and pineapple alternately onto skewers and place them on a platter.
  6. Arrange the skewers on the hot grill and cook for 2 to 3 minutes per side until the shrimp is opaque and cooked through.
  7. Turn the grill to low heat and brush the sauce on both sides of the skewers.
  8. Transfer the skewers to a serving platter
  9. Sprinkle with cilantro and sesame seeds as garnish.
  10. Optional: Cut the pineapple into similar-sized chunks as the shrimp for even grilling.
  11. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes before grilling to prevent burning.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 pound jumbo shrimp
  • ½ fresh pineapple, cored and cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  • skewers
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds (Optional)
